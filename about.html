About Us




Our Message

At JxGTech Incorporated, we are passionate about driving innovation and fostering creativity in the tech community. As leaders in technology solutions and community engagement, we pride ourselves on creating platforms that empower developers, thinkers, and innovators to collaborate and build the future. This year, we are thrilled to host Hackathon #1738, a premier event that brings together tech enthusiasts from around the globe. Whether you're a seasoned coder, a curious beginner, or a visionary entrepreneur, Hackathon #1738 is your chance to solve real-world problems, connect with like-minded individuals, and showcase your skills in a dynamic and supportive environment. Join us as we celebrate the power of technology and teamwork to shape tomorrow!

Our History
Founded in 2010, JTech Incorporated began as a small startup with a big vision: to harness the power of technology to solve complex challenges and inspire innovation. Over the years, we've grown into a trusted leader in tech solutions, known for our commitment to excellence and community engagement. Our journey has been marked by groundbreaking projects, collaborative partnerships, and a passion for fostering talent. Hosting events like Hackathon #1738 is a testament to our dedication to empowering the next generation of tech innovators.
